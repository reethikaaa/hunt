8. What has the suspect’s behavior been like before and after the crime? Answer: Before the crime, the suspect was reported to be anxious and aggressive. After the crime, the suspect exhibited signs of nervousness and attempted to avoid law enforcement.
9. Are there any surveillance videos or photographs that capture the crime or the suspect? Answer: A surveillance camera across the street captured a person matching the suspect’s description entering and leaving the victim’s case around the time of the crime.

EMPLOYMENT OPPORTUNITIES

Roles:

  • Investigating Officers
  • Sub-Inspectors
  • Constables
  • Forensic Experts
  • Cyber Crime Investigators
  • Intelligence Officers
  • Administrative Staff
  • Technical Staff
  • Language Experts
  • Legal Advisors

Eligibility:

  • Indian citizenship
  • Age limits (varies by role)
  • Educational qualifications (graduation, post-graduation, etc.)
  • Physical fitness
  • Background checks

Selection Process:

  • Written examinations
  • Physical efficiency tests
  • Interviews
  • Medical examinations
  • Verification of documents

Training:

  • Basic training at CBI Academy
  • Specialized training in investigation, forensic science, etc.
  • On-the-job training

Career Growth:

  • Promotions based on performance and seniority
  • Opportunities for specialization
  • Participation in international investigations and training

Benefits:

  • Competitive salary and allowances
  • Government accommodation
  • Medical facilities
  • Retirement benefits
  • Opportunities for foreign postings

CBI Recruitment:

  • Notifications on official website (cbi.gov.in)
  • Advertisements in national newspapers
  • Online applications

Note: The selection process, eligibility criteria, and benefits may vary depending on the specific role and recruitment notification.
